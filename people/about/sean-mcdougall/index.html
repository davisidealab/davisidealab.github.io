Sean McDougall

Staff Research Associate I

University of California, Davis

Sean McDougall is the assistant lab manager and analyst at the UC Davis IDeA Lab. At the IDeA Lab he leads the analyst team to ensure that incoming data is quickly and expertly analyzed and delivered to research labs across the United States. In addition he assists the engineering and research teams in day to day operations and projects spanning many months.
